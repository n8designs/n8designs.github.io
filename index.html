Melontime

  • 1 month

  • team of 2

  • assignment

Melontime is a physical product design that was self fabricated and physically made with functionality and appealing design in mind within a team of two. Melontime is a home decor piece that's meant to conceptually tell time by acting as a 30 second timer, by rocking back and forth. This project was made using a variety of skills of conceptualizing and ideating of the design, 3D-Modelling and Arduino, as well as hand-skills of fabrication. Although the functionality of a thirty second timer is a bit absurd, the true point of Melontime is a homage to the Dutch designer, RAW COLOR, by adding more colorful pieces within the home, as well as to communicate joy to others.

Hatched

  • 2 months

  • team of 5

  • assignment

Hatched is a short film curated by a team of 5 within the span of 2 months. This project consisted of us utilizing our skills of character design, storyboarding and storybuilding along with gaining new skills in 3D Modelling and animation using software such as AutoDesk Maya. This project allowed me to explore different 3D modelling and rendering styles and has affected how I have animated ever since, giving me an entrance to animation concepts such as squash and stretch, anticipation and follow through when a character does an action.

Agoda Feature Redesign: Plans

  • 1 week

  • individual

  • assignment

The agoda feature redesign was an individual project to either redesign or add a feature to an existing service. This project had helped me explore and define and curate a user experience, identifying problems and solving them through ideation. Keeping the user experience in mind means putting yourself in the user's shoes and understanding the different processes of getting the user, or the designer, on the same page. This project allowed me to explore more within UI/UX, where I had successfully created a system easily accessible and understood by users.
